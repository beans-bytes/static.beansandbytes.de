  • Customer/client interaction level: While not in the traditional sense of "customers," scientists often interact with community members, interview subjects, and stakeholders who are involved in or affected by the research.

  • Communication complexity: Communication is complex, requiring clear articulation of research findings both in written forms (e.g., journals) and verbally (e.g., presentations).

3. Additional Factors:

  • Cultural sensitivity requirements: High degree of cultural sensitivity is essential, as research often spans diverse groups and sensitive topics.

  • Conflict resolution needs: Some conflict resolution skills may be needed, particularly in team settings or during fieldwork with varied participants.

  • Leadership/influence components: Leadership may be required within research teams, guiding junior researchers, or influencing public and academic discourse based on research findings.
